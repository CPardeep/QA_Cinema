Standard Seating

Description
Our standard screens offers comfort, engagement and 50 ft x 25 ft size screens for your viewing pleasure. This is fun for groups of all sizes and are the most popular choice amongst our movie goers.

Our classically designed 150 seater theatres are made with comfort and style in mind.
